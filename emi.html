I AM THE BOX STATE QUEEN

Hi guys! My name is Emigine Sisa, aka Emigine or The Box State Queen! Yes it's clear that I am a proud Colorado native born and raised in the Box State my entire life. I've traveled to numerous states and of course there's nothing like home. Except California I loveee it there! Anyways I'll tell you guys a little bit about myself and I would love for you all to continue to watch this journey with me as I build my foundation up from nothing. So tune in and welcome to my website!

I was born in Wheat Ridge Colorado April 15th, 1997. I have 3 older sisters who I care about dearly. They're from Indiana & North Carolina but we've all been settled in Colorado for 20 plus years. I was raised by my two amazing parents who are originally from The Democratic Republic of Congo. Some of our ancestors were from Portugal as well. Growing up being this kind of an African-American child had life a bit different. The mix between the African and American culture is what I feel made me into a unique artist. I love many arts! It is my passion. When I found out how much I loved poetry that was when I was in elementary school. I started writing all kinds of poetry including haikus, free verse poems just expressing what I saw at the time and did in life and then it turned into deep thoughts & internal feelings and then I started writing poems that rhymed. This turned out be my favorite style of poetry! I couldn't stop with the limericks and sonnets. Then I started writing lyrics and beatboxing in 6th grade and I just kept going from there.
